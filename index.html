What are your gift card options?

+

We offer special gift cards to give your loved ones an unforgettable relaxation experience. You can either choose a specific spa treatment or load any amount you wish onto the card. Payment can be easily made through a secure link we will send you, without visiting the location. The gift card can be delivered as an electronic voucher (e-voucher) via email or through courier service within Baku. On the day the gift card is used, our guest has the privilege of enjoying all spa center facilities free of charge.

What is the daily spa entry price and what services are included?

+

For a perfect spa experience, our daily entry price is 65 AZN on weekdays and 75 AZN on weekends and holidays. This price includes access to the indoor pool, jacuzzi, steam room, sauna, relaxation area, fitness center, and personal locker service.

Are there separate hours for ladies and gentlemen?

+

There are no specific time restrictions for ladies or gentlemen at our spa center; the fitness and indoor pool areas are intended for shared use by all our guests. However, to ensure our guests' comfort, separate saunas and steam rooms for ladies and gentlemen are available in the spa zone located on the 6th floor.

Can I get information about membership packages?

+

For those who choose a healthy lifestyle, we have different membership categories including Quartz, Silver Lite, Silver, Gold, and Diamond. For detailed information, you can visit linktr.ee/Fairmontspa or contact us via WhatsApp for your questions.

Could you provide information about your operating hours?

+

Our fitness center is open 24 hours for hotel guests, Gold and Diamond members. All other spa and relaxation zones are at your service daily from 06:30 to 22:00.

Is there parking available?

Yes, complimentary parking service is available for our guests and members using spa treatments.
